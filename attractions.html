Attractions

Tour A

Tour A, costing P1200 will take you across the islands, visiting these main points of interest: Big Lagoon, Small Lagoon, Secret Lagoon, Shimizu Island and Seven Commandos Beach. Big, Small and Secret Lagoons offer clear water with many fishes perfect for swimming. Shimizu Island offers white-sand beaches, snorkeling with corals and limestone cliffs. Your day will end at Seven Commandos Beach, which has a long white sand beach, and a bar that will serve you refreshing drinks.

Tour C

Tour C which costs P1400 takes you to Helicopter Island first, with a white sand and rocky beach. Next is Matinloc Shrine is an abandoned convent surrounded by legend. Lastly, the tour takes you to the Secret, Hidden and Talisay Beach with clear water and white sand.

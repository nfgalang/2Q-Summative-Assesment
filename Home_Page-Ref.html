About us

Hi and welcome to our website about El Nido, Palawan! Our group is consisted of Nuntius Diego Galang, Albert Jose Marcos, and Jaden Jose De Guzman. Nuncio (Nuntius Diego) is our leader, giving us directions on what our website should look like. AJ (Albert Jose) is our programmer having the most knowledge about programming in general. Jaden (Jaden Jose) is our researcher, giving us intel about El Nido's places, cultures, and etc. We picked El Nido due to the fact that it has nice beaches, multiple islands, and that none of us have ever been to El Nido.
